Support for professionals

OnLook welcomes collaboration with professionals from all sectors — from local government units and barangays to healthcare workers, social services, and rescue teams. Your expertise plays a vital role in the early response, coordinated search, and continued care for missing or cognitively impaired individuals in Laguna. We believe that a connected community of professionals can create faster, more compassionate outcomes.

“Collaboration isn’t just a method — it’s a lifeline for those who are missing and those who miss them.”

How Professionals Can Help

  • Join as a Partner Agency: Register your organization to access specialized tools and resources designed for professionals involved in search and rescue operations.
  • Access Resources for LGUs and First Responders: Utilize our curated materials, training modules, and best practices to enhance your response capabilities.
  • Report Sightings Efficiently: Use our streamlined reporting system to log sightings and share critical information with the community and other professionals.
  • Coordinate with OnLook Volunteers: Work alongside our dedicated volunteers to organize search efforts and provide support to affected families.
