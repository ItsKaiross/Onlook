We're here to help

There are a number of ways we we can help

Getting Help When You’re Lost

If you or a loved one is missing, it can feel overwhelming. OnLook provides clear steps on how to reconnect with your family and access immediate support, including local resources and community assistance.

Your Legal Rights During a Search

Being missing doesn't mean you’re in trouble. This guide explains how the authorities and OnLook approach missing cases with respect to your rights, and how we ensure you are safe throughout the process.

How We Check In on Your Safety

When you’re facing a difficult time, staying connected is essential. OnLook has a system to reach out and confirm your well-being. Through discreet messages, we ensure you’re safe and can offer any needed support, while also respecting your privacy. This helps you stay in touch with those who care and gives you the space to feel secure.

Caring for Your Mental and Emotional Health

Experiencing a disappearance or cognitive impairment can take a toll on your mental health. OnLook connects individuals to mental health professionals and resources, ensuring that your well-being is prioritized during difficult times.

Staying Secure and Making Safe Decisions

If you feel the need to leave home, it’s essential to stay safe. OnLook provides guidance on how to make informed decisions, protect yourself, and stay in touch with those who can help you.

Finding the Right Words to Share Your Story

Opening up about what’s happening in your life can be hard. This section provides advice on how to express yourself to others—whether it’s a family member, a professional, or someone in your community—so you can get the support you need.
